30th Anniversary Celebration

June 15, 2025

Dear Students, Parents, and Staff,

We are thrilled to announce that Cruise Steps School is celebrating its 30th anniversary this year! Since our establishment in 1987 EC, we have been committed to delivering quality education and setting high standards for schools in our area and across the country.

This year marks a special milestone in our school's history, and we invite everyone to join us for a grand community celebration on June 30th. The event will bring together current and former students, parents, and staff members to reconnect and share memories.

Led by professional educational experts renowned in the field, our school continues to deliver quality education that evolves with the needs and challenges of the time. This celebration is not just about looking back at our achievements, but also about looking forward to an even brighter future.

We humbly invite all members of our school community to participate in this joyous occasion. Should you have any questions, please don't hesitate to contact the school office.

Thank you for being part of our journey!
